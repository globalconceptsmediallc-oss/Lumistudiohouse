LUMI STUDIO

Studio

Lumi Studio is a creative house devoted to gentle stories, thoughtful worlds, and characters who help children and families explore courage, friendship, and quiet strength.

Lumi Studio began with a simple idea: even a small light can help us take a careful step forward. Through calm storytelling and emotionally grounded worldbuilding, we create journeys that move gently, feel safe, and leave room for reflection.

What We Create

Stories — gentle narratives that move with calm, meaning, and emotional honesty.

Worlds — places where quiet moments matter and every step reveals something new.

Characters — companions whose journeys reflect growth, courage, belonging, and care.

Current World

Lumi & Friends is a growing story world centered on a small otter and his companions as they move through Lanternwood, discovering courage, friendship, and the light they carry within.

Meet Lumi & Friends Explore Lanternwood

How We Think

We believe children’s stories can be beautiful, gentle, and emotionally meaningful without needing to become loud or rushed. Lumi Studio is built around the idea that quiet stories still have the power to stay with us — and sometimes guide us.
