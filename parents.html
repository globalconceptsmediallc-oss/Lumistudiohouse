Parents & Educators

Stories that help children explore courage, friendship, kindness, and emotional growth through Lumi’s world.

Why Lumi Stories Matter

Lumi stories are written to give children a gentle place to explore feelings, friendships, uncertainty, and courage. Through simple adventures and meaningful moments, young readers can see that emotions are normal, growth takes time, and even small acts of bravery matter.

Rather than teaching lessons in a heavy-handed way, Lumi’s world invites children to discover those ideas naturally through story, character, and imagination.

Reading Together

Reading Lumi stories together can open the door to calm and meaningful conversations between children and the adults who care for them. Parents, grandparents, teachers, and caregivers can use the stories to talk about feelings, friendship, problem-solving, and confidence in ways that feel safe and approachable.

Helpful questions to ask while reading:

  • How do you think Lumi feels here?
  • What would you do in that moment?
  • Has anything like this ever happened to you?
  • What helped the character keep going?

Classroom & Homeschool Use

Lumi stories can be used in classrooms, reading groups, homeschool lessons, and quiet reading time. The themes are well suited for language arts discussions, social emotional learning, and creative response activities.

  • Read-aloud story sessions
  • Emotion and character discussion prompts
  • Creative drawing or journal responses
  • Small group conversation activities
  • Reflection on courage, kindness, and friendship

Emotional Learning

At the heart of Lumi’s stories is emotional learning. Children see characters face uncertainty, overcome fear, support one another, and grow through experience. These moments can help young readers better understand their own feelings and build empathy for others.

Themes often explored in Lumi’s world include:

  • Courage in unfamiliar situations
  • Friendship and belonging
  • Patience and perseverance
  • Kindness and compassion
  • Confidence through small steps

The Characters and Their Strengths

Each character in the Lumi universe reflects different strengths that children may recognize in themselves or wish to grow.

  • Lumi - courage, curiosity, and heart
  • Rowan - clever thinking and quick insight
  • Fern - kindness, care, and gentleness
  • Pebble - patience, steadiness, and calm
  • Wren - wonder, energy, and exploration
  • Alder - wisdom, perspective, and guidance

A Small Steady Moment

Sometimes the best way to continue a story is simply to pause.

You might try this together after reading a Lumi story.

Place one hand over your heart.

Take one slow breath in.

Notice something steady near you.

Take one slow breath out.

Take one more careful breath.

The forest in the story does not disappear. It simply grows quiet. You can grow quiet too.
