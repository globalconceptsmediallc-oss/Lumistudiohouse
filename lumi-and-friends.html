Lumi & Friends

In a quiet hollow where lantern light meets the evening sky, Lumi and his friends discover courage, friendship, and the quiet strength carried within.

Meet Lumi

Lumi is a thoughtful otter who once believed the lantern he carried was the source of his courage. As his journey unfolds, he begins to discover something deeper — that the light guiding him may have been inside him all along.

The Friends

Rowan Steady and thoughtful, Rowan often sees the path when others feel unsure.
Fern Curious and observant, Fern notices the quiet wonders others might miss.
Pebble Grounded and dependable, Pebble reminds everyone that strength does not need to be loud.
Wren Lighthearted and quick, Wren brings energy and joy when the road feels heavy.
Alder Wise and patient, Alder understands that every path reveals itself in time.

Lanternwood

Lanternwood is a world of winding paths, quiet rivers, glowing stones, hidden trails, and evening light that softens everything it touches. Each step forward reveals something new — about the world, about friendship, and about the light already within.
