Lumi & Friends

In a quiet hollow where lantern light meets the evening sky, a small otter and his friends discover courage, kindness, and the light they carry within.

Meet Lumi

Lumi is a thoughtful otter who once believed the lantern he carried was the source of his courage. Along his journey, he begins to discover something deeper—that the light guiding him may have been inside him all along.

The Friends

Rowan Steady and thoughtful, Rowan often sees the path before others do.
Fern Curious and observant, Fern notices the small wonders others miss.
Pebble Grounded and dependable, Pebble reminds everyone that strength can be quiet.
Wren Lighthearted and quick, Wren brings joy when the journey feels heavy.
Alder Wise and patient, Alder understands that every path takes time.

Lanternwood

Lanternwood is a place of winding paths, gentle rivers, and evening light that glows softly at dusk. Here, each small step forward reveals something new—about the world, and about oneself.
